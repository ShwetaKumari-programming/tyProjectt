About us

Running under the guidance of a true visionary, Mr. Sanjay Patel, CEO of the company, Elmech Industries has come a long way since its inception in the year 2004. With the sincere dedication of a team of experts the company has spread its wings in the worldwide markets. Originally, we are headquartered at Ahmedabad, Gujarat.

Factory & Infrastructure

We operate on a wide-spread infrastructural area of around 500 sq. yard, wherein we have installed all modern amenities and equipment, which is required to manufacture the products that we offer. In addition to this, we have a production capacity of 1000 Pics./per month and we have two factories.

Our Manufacturing System

We are engaged in manufacturing special purpose motors as per customer requirement like tailor made Motor. We are assisted by a team of experts, who can assure supply of a wide range from 110V to 480V 3-Phase & 40 Hz to 100 Hz frequency. In addition to this, we also engaged in manufacturing Electric Motor with suitable mechanical dimensions & arrangement as per customer requirement. Moreover, we have specialized in manufacturing special type motors for SPM machines.

Quality Policy

We never compromise on the quality of good that we offer to the clients. In addition to this, we take pride in mentioning that we have attained ISO certification for offering the best-in-class products to our clients that too while catering to their specific requirements in a time-bound manner.
